History

THE BEGINNING OF A GREAT CAREER
La Hacienda restaurant is the third venture of two brothers who, with more than 20 years of experience, have managed to concentrate a wide variety of flavors in various Jalisco specialty dishes. In March 2020, the world was experiencing a situation that completely changed our lifestyle (COVID-19). Despite the difficult situation, La Hacienda Restaurant begins its services only with takeout: After that and respecting the health regulations established by our authorities, in May 2020 the doors of our facilities open. As a group we are happy and proud of our Mexican roots. We are here to generate more stories by offering the best of our food, always serving our guests with humility
